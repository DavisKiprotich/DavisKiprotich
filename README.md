## About Me

I am an Electrical and Telecommunications Engineer from Moi University who builds practical digital products and real-world technical solutions. My work brings together software delivery, engineering thinking, and field awareness across web platforms, mobile apps, connected systems, and Power &amp; IT environments.

## What I Do Best

- Build clean, responsive web platforms, company websites, and product-facing interfaces
- Ship mobile applications with product structure, Play Store readiness, and source-backed delivery
- Support systems integration involving routers, monitoring setups, fuel probes, DC meters, and site visibility
- Approach software with an engineering lens that values reliability, deployment context, usability, and maintainability

## How I Work

I like solving practical problems with solutions that can hold up in real use, not just in ideal demos. Whether I am building a website, shipping a mobile product, or supporting an integration workflow, I focus on clarity, reliability, and execution that makes sense in the environment where the work will actually live.

## What Makes Me Different

My software work is backed by engineering context. I do not only think about screens and code. I also think about uptime, field conditions, infrastructure, devices, monitoring, and how a system behaves once people depend on it. That combination helps me contribute to both the visible product and the technical environment around it.
